About Meyzam Engineering

Meyzam Engineering and Projects is an engineering company founded in 2023. We are a small office that designs electrical plans and schemes for various types of buildings, including high-standard housing, residential condominiums, office buildings, commercial buildings, religious buildings, mixed-use buildings, industrial warehouses, and many more.

We manage projects from conception to execution, including diagrams, tables, and all necessary documentation for system functionality. We also develop structured cabling projects, CCTV, access control, security, audio-visual, and fire alarm and detection projects, as well as electrical discharge protection systems (SPDA in Brazilian Portuguese).

Our services include technical documentation (laudos in Portuguese), construction follow-up (acompanhamento de obras), and various other electrical engineering services for buildings. We prioritize time efficiency and high quality in all our projects.

Our mission is to deliver top-quality engineering solutions with integrity and excellence. We envision a future where our innovative and reliable services set the standard in the industry.

Mission

To provide high-quality, efficient, and innovative engineering solutions that meet the diverse needs of our clients and contribute to the development of safe and sustainable buildings.

Operating in São Paulo City, Brazil, we use CAD software and BIM methodology to develop detailed project information before construction begins, ensuring precision and efficiency.
