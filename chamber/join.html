NP Membership

  • Event Access: Free or discounted access to chamber-hosted events and workshops.
  • Basic Training Sessions: Access to introductory training sessions and workshops at no cost or at a discounted rate.
  • Directory Listing: Standard listing in the chamber directory, which includes contact information and a brief description.
  • Advertising: Mention in chamber newsletters and occasional promotional materials.
  • Event Discounts: Small discounts on event registration fees.
  • Networking Opportunities: Invitations to general networking events and mixers.
  • Community Support: Opportunities to collaborate with other non-profits and participate in community outreach initiatives.
×

Bronze Membership

  • Event Access: Free or discounted access to chamber-hosted events, workshops, and seminars.
  • Advanced Training Sessions: Access to more specialized training sessions and workshops at no cost or at a discounted rate.
  • Enhanced Directory Listing: Prominent listing in the chamber directory with additional features like logo and extended description.
  • Advertising: Regular mentions in chamber newsletters, social media, and promotional materials.
  • Event Discounts: Larger discounts on event registration fees compared to NP Membership.
  • Networking Opportunities: Invitations to exclusive networking events and mixers with industry-specific focus.
  • Community Support: Opportunities for collaboration on chamber-led projects and initiatives.
  • Spotlight Feature: Occasional spotlight features on the chamber’s website or newsletter.
×

Silver Membership

  • Event Access: Complimentary access to all chamber-hosted events, workshops, and seminars.
  • Premium Training Sessions: Access to premium training sessions and workshops, including exclusive member-only sessions.
  • Premium Directory Listing: Highlighted listing in the chamber directory with enhanced visibility, including logo, extended description, and featured position.
  • Advertising: Regular features in chamber newsletters, social media, and targeted promotional campaigns.
  • Event Discounts: Significant discounts or possibly free tickets for chamber events.
  • Networking Opportunities: Invitations to high-profile networking events, including industry-specific meetups and roundtables.
  • Community Support: Active involvement in chamber-led community outreach initiatives and projects.
  • Spotlight Feature: Featured spotlight section on the chamber’s homepage and in newsletters.
  • Promotional Opportunities: Opportunities for exclusive promotional partnerships with the chamber.
×

Gold Membership

  • Event Access: Complimentary and priority access to all chamber-hosted events, workshops, and exclusive seminars.
  • Elite Training Sessions: Access to elite training sessions and workshops, including private one-on-one sessions with experts.
  • Elite Directory Listing: Top-tier listing in the chamber directory with maximum visibility, including prominent logo placement, detailed description, and top spot.
  • Advertising: Prime advertising spots in chamber newsletters, social media, and high-visibility promotional materials.
  • Event Discounts: Exclusive discounts or complimentary passes for all chamber events, plus VIP access.
  • Networking Opportunities: VIP invitations to all networking events, including special industry roundtables and exclusive mixers.
  • Community Support: Premier involvement in high-profile chamber-led community projects and initiatives.
  • Spotlight Feature: Prominent spotlight feature on the chamber’s homepage, newsletters, and social media.
  • Promotional Opportunities: Premium promotional opportunities, including featured partnerships, sponsorships, and exclusive content.
  • Consulting Services: Access to specialized consulting services or business advice from chamber experts.
